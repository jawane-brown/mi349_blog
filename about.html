About Mechanic Mags

Mechanic Mags was created to provide car enthusiasts with a place where they can find all kinds of information pertaining to maintaining and working on vehicles. Our founder, Jawane Brown Jr., is a student at Michigan State University studying Information Science and a fellow car enthusiast. He has owned multiple vehicles across the spectrum, from Jaguars to Cadillacs. He has had his ups and downs with vehicle maintenance, and because of his struggles, he decided to create Mechanic Mags, your go-to place for tips and concerns about vehicle maintenance.

We, at Mechanic Mags, strive to provide our community with the most accurate, up-to-date, and ethical information pertaining to vehicle maintenance. We know the struggle of not knowing, and we want to help you figure it out at all times. We are here for you.

Our Values:

  • Accuracy: We ensure that the information we provide is reliable and based on factual knowledge.
  • Expertise: Our team consists of experienced mechanics and car enthusiasts who share their valuable insights.
  • Transparency: We are committed to being open and honest with our readers, providing clear and unbiased information.
  • Community: We strive to foster a supportive and engaging community where car enthusiasts can share their experiences and learn from one another.
  • Continuous Learning: We are dedicated to staying updated with the latest advancements in automotive technology and sharing that knowledge with our audience.
