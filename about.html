About Mechanic Mags

Mechanic Mags was created to provide car enthusiasts with a place where they can find all kinds of informatio pertaining to maintaining and working on vehicles. Our founder, Jawane Brown Jr. is a student at Michigan State University studying Information Science, and a fellow car enthusiast. He has owned mutltiple vehicles across the spectrum, from Jaguars to Cadillacs. He has had his ups and downs with vehicle maintenance, and because of his struggle, he decided to create Mechanic Mags, your go to place for tips and concerns about vehicle maintenance.
