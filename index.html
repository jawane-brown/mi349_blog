10 Essential Tools Every Car Enthusiast Should Have In Their Garage

Jawane Brown
Dec 8, 2021

Having the correct tools in your garage is crucial for anyone who considers themself a car enthusiast and for those who want to take their automotive hobbies to the next level. Whether you're a beginner or have been in the game for some years, having these tools in your arsenal will save you much time and empower you with the ability to tackle nearly any automotive project that presents itself.

The 10 Essential Tools Are: 1) Socket Set. This is good to tighten or loosen bolts and njts with ease, making repairs more effiecient. 2)Wrenche. A set of combination wrenches are useful when tightening or loosing fasteners in tighter spaces. 3)Screwdriver Set. You can never go wrong with a set of screwdrivers. They are useful for a range of things, from removing interior panels to working om more complex parts. 4)Pliers. These are useful for providing a firm grip on small objects. 5)Torque Wrench. These are extremely useful when ensuring the correct tighness of the bolts, usually to manufacturer specs. 6) Jack and Jack Stands. How else are you going to get under the car? Seriously there are essential for safety and stability when working under the vehicle. 7? Multimeter. Extremely useful for diagnosing and troubleshooting electrical issues. 8)Oil Filter Wrench. Changing the oil is routine maintenance, and you must change the oil filter. 9) Brake Bleeder Kit. This allows you to remove air bubbles from the brake system after performing a brake job. 10)Code Reader: These help you identify and understand what the check engine light means and other error codes. This helps diagnose potential issues and provides potential solutions.

Maintance Tips when Modifying Your 2016+ Cadillac ATS 2.0T

Jawane Brown
Jun 1, 2023

The Cadillac ATS 2.0T is one of General Motors' luxury compact sedans. It offers a combination of stylish design cues, impressive performance, and advanced technology. The exterior design is sleek and modern, showcasing clean lines, sharp lights, and a bold grille that embodies Cadillac's distinctive styling.

I've owned a 2017 Cadillac ATS 2.0t for the past two years now, and after modifying it and learning the car on the daily basis, I can provide some insights into maintenance for this vehicle. Starting with the basics, if you do not have a manual with your vehicle, you can find it online. The manual has the maintenacne schedule, showing owners the recommended intervals by mileage for certain components of the vehicle. These are extremely useful, but as owners experience the car more, we begin to find preventative maintenace tasks. When I first modified my vehicle, I did little to no research on the vehicle, which was my first fail. I installed a cold air intake, catted downpipe, ECU tune, and transmission tune. With that, I quickly fell in love with everything about the vehicle, for all of 2 months. I was in the middle of my hour drive from home to school when my vehicle lost all power, smoking from the tailpipe and under the hood. After researching at this point, I found out that Cadillac's piston rings in the LTG engine are extremely weak, even from factory. Adding power and increasing airflow resulted in the piston rings melting, allowing oil to fill up my third cylinder, finally cracking the long block. What did I do next? Bought a new engine and tried again. If you plan to modify your LTG product, (ATS, CTS, Camaro), this is what you NEED to do before. First, make sure regular maintenance is up to date. Next, purchase a high performing oil catch can system. With the vehicle bieng turbocharged and having 20 pounds of boost from factory, oil flows within the air and into the intake manifold, called blowby. Furthermore, consider purchasing and installing forged internals, such as pistons. There are forged pistons on the market that can handle over 1000hp. With these products, you will be able to install Stage 1 modifications and enjoy the sound of a turbo whistle and exhilirating acceleration throughout the entire rev range. Turbo boost will be at your disposal.
